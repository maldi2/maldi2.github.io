About Me

Welcome to my academic webpage. I am an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Virginia Commonwealth University. My academic work focuses on algebraic and geometric techniques to solve mathematical problems inspired by physics. I have also worked on asymptotic combinatorics, the foundations of real analysis, and mathematical education.

I am a member of the VCU Geometry Group, of the Mid-Atlantic Regional Math Alliance , and of the Virginia Academy of Science.

Research Interests
