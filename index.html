About Me

Welcome to my academic webpage. I am an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU). My academic work focuses on exploring complex mathematical structures and their applications.

In my work I use algebraic techniques to solve mathematical problems inspired by Physics.

  • Real Analysis
