About Me

Welcome to my academic webpage. I am an associate professor in the Department of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU). My academic work focuses on algebraic and geometric techniques to solve mathematical problems inspired by physics. I have also worked on asymptotic combinatorics, the foundations of real analysis, and mathematical education.

I am a member of the VCU Geometry Group

Research Interests

  • Mathematical Physics
  • Geometry
  • Algebra
  • Number Theory
  • Computational Complexity
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Real Analysis
  • Mathematical Education
